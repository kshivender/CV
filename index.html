T0 — Profile
Who I am

Two years, six completed projects, eighteen fields mapped.

As a Project Geophysicist, I work end-to-end on seismic interpretation for both onshore and offshore exploration blocks — seismic and well data conditioning, well correlation, well-to-seismic tie, VSP analysis, horizon picking, and detailed fault mapping for structural and stratigraphic evaluation.

I contribute to isopach, isochron and pay maps, multi-attribute analysis, spectral decomposition, thin-sand identification, prospect delineation, POS-based ranking, and structure-model building for static modelling — work that feeds directly into exploration decisions and prospect maturation.

I've contributed G&G interpretation for static modelling of four Cambay Basin fields — Akholjuni, Vadatal, Anklav and Kathana — and supported techno-economic evaluation across 14 fields.
